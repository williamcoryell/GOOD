The GOOD Detector is an advanced AI-powered sentiment and opinion analysis tool that scours the vast landscape of the internet, from news articles and social media platforms like Twitter and Reddit, to uncover and analyze opinions on trending topics. Built with a BERT transformer specifically fine-tuned for social media, the GOOD Detector seamlessly captures the nuances of online conversations, identifying keywords, sentiments, and contextual relationships in real-time. Meanwhile, a T5 model expertly interprets the more formal tone and structure of news articles, summarizing viewpoints and forming comprehensive insights on current events. Whether you're a brand looking to stay ahead of public opinion, a researcher studying social trends, or a curious individual wanting to understand the pulse of online discussions, the GOOD Detector provides a clear, unbiased breakdown of the collective internet voice.

ABOUT G.O.O.D.

Creators

Ethan Cheung, William Coryell, and Myung Lee. We are seniors from Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology (TJHSST) in Alexandria, Virginia.

The Project

This is our senior research project in the Systems Lab at TJHSST. We wanted to tackle the problem of echo chambers in online spaces, which has become more prevalent in recent years. We designed G.O.O.D. to be a tool one could use to see if they are in an echo chamber, by examining many other opinions and perspectives. By seeing other perspectives on issues, you can get a better understanding of issues, rather than having what you already believe reinforced by the news and social media posts you constantly see.
